New Set Pre-sale

Sword & Shield Expansion Pack Paradigm Trigger

The next Japanese set, S12 Paradigm Trigger, has been revealed during the Champions League 2023 Yokohama Livestream.
This is the fourth main expansion set of the 2022 Sword & Shield TCG, and the final main expansion pack set of the year and generation before the Scarlet & Violet TCG series arrives in 2023 (the last Japanese Sword & Shield set will be S12a High Class Pack VSTAR Universe). Paradigm Trigger will be part of our international Sword & Shield - Silver Tempest set.

Trick or Trade BOOster Bundle

The Pokémon Company International has announced an upcoming Halloween campaign in which Trainers will be invited to incorporate Pokémon into their spooky celebrations through themed activations and merchandise across the franchise. This set will provide parents and children with a fun and innovative alternative to candy for trick-or-treating when it hits participating grocery stores and mass retailers on September 1, 2022.

Pokemon GO Premier Deck Holder Collection

First is the Pokémon TCG: Pokémon GO Premier Deck Holder Collection—Dragonite VSTAR. This product includes Dragonite V (SWSH235) and Dragonite VSTAR (SWSH236) promot cards . Will be available on September 30, 2022. This is the last Pokemon GO-related product that has been planned. Finally, this item includes a deck holder in the shape of a Premier Ball, as well as 9 booster packs for the Pokemon GO TCG set.

Sword & Shield - Lost Origin

Pokemon and characters from the Hisui region from the video game Pokemon Legends: Arceus will be included in this set, comparable to Astral Radiance. Furthermore, the Lost Zone mechanic has been returned. Pokémon VSTAR cards, Giratina VSTAR, Aerodactyl VSTAR, Magnezone VSTAR, Drapion VSTAR, Hisuian Goodra VSTAR, Hisuian Zoroark VSTAR, and 1 VMAX card named Kyurem VMAX are all included.

Origin Forme Dialga VSTAR / Origin Forme Palkia VSTAR

The eagerly awaited Origin Forme Pokémon VSTAR Premium Collection has been listed by a distributor. Many fans had predicted this would happen since Astral Radiance did not include the Full Art cards of Origin Forme Dialga V and Origin Forme Palkia V. They should appear in this product, as expected. It was confirmed earlier this week that these two Full Arts would be promos.

It also includes "etched" VSTAR cards of each individual Pokemon; it's unclear whether these are exact copies of the Astral Radiance versions or include new, different artwork.

V Battle Decks - Deoxys V / Zeraora V

Deoxys & Zeraora are the next V Battle Decks. These decks are 'Level 1' similar to the previously released Corviknight V / Lycanroc V / V Battle Decks - Rayquaza V / Noivern V, V Battle Decks - Victini V / Gardevoir V and V Battle Decks - Venusaur V / Blastoise V.

Deoxys V and Zeraora V should likely originate from the Japanese VSTAR & VMAX High Class Deck Zeraora / Deoxys. As of now not confirmed if it contains any new artwork.
